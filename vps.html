Common questions

Do I need a VPS?

You don't strictly need one — Proof Gold works on any computer running MT4. But without a VPS, the EA only trades when your computer is on and connected. A VPS means the EA never misses a session.

Can I use my existing VPS?

Yes. Any Windows VPS that can run MT4 will work. ForexVPS is our recommendation because MT4 comes pre-installed, but you can use any provider.

Can I run other EAs on the same VPS?

Yes. The Basic plan has enough resources for multiple MT4 instances and several EAs running simultaneously.

How do I check on my trades?

Connect to your VPS from any device — laptop, phone, or tablet using a Remote Desktop app. You'll see your MT4 with Proof Gold running, the dashboard showing your trades, and you can disconnect without interrupting anything.
