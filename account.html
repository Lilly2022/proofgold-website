How Activations Work

Each activation = one MT4 account number. You can install the EA on unlimited computers (home PC, laptop, VPS) — it only counts as one activation per MT4 account.

Need to move to a new broker? Click "Remove" on the old account, then start the EA on your new MT4 account. The activation transfers instantly.

Need help? Email support@proof.trading with your license key.
